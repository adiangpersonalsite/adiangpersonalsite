ABOUT ME



Who I Am

I am Adiang Goc, a 21 years old South Sudanese residing in Addis Ababa, owner of Selam Tech Store and a member of Ethio Digital Academy. As a young graphic designer with a passion for digital marketing and architecture, I thrive at the intersection of creativity and strategy. My journey began with a fascination for visual storytelling, which led me to pursue graphic design. Over the years, I honed my skills in digital marketing, learning how to effectively communicate ideas and engage audiences across various platforms. My architectural background gives me a unique perspective, allowing me to blend functionality with aesthetic appeal in my designs. I am constantly seeking new challenges that push my creative boundaries and enable me to make a meaningful impact in the digital landscape.

As a passionate graphic designer, I bring creative visions to life through compelling visuals and innovative branding solutions. In my role as a digital marketer, I leverage my design skills to craft engaging campaigns that resonate with audiences and drive results. My architectural background enhances my ability to approach projects with a keen eye for detail and functionality, allowing me to create designs that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also practical.
